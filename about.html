About

The people and approach behind AC Intelligence.

AC Intelligence is an AI consulting and implementation firm built to help businesses apply AI in practical, measurable ways. We focus on systems that get built, used, and improved, not abstract strategy.

Co-Founders

Built by founders focused on practical AI implementation and real business impact.

AC Intelligence is led by Kaiden Amaro and Frankie Campbell, two founders focused on helping businesses implement AI in ways that improve operations, strengthen execution, and support growth. Together, they bring hands-on experience across automation, marketing, and applied AI systems, with work that has supported billion-dollar companies and active automation across more than 20 businesses.

How the company is built

Serious about implementation, not just ideas.

AC Intelligence is built around a simple belief: AI only matters if it improves how the business actually runs. Everything we take on is judged by whether it can be implemented, measured, and improved over time.

Implementation over theory

We focus on systems that get built and used in the day-to-day, not slide decks that sit on a shelf.

Business-first judgment

We start with operational needs and business goals before deciding where AI belongs, and where it does not.

Built for the long term

We stay involved after launch so the systems keep delivering as the business grows and changes.

AC Intelligence works with businesses wherever the right opportunity exists, bringing practical implementation, modern systems thinking, and a serious commitment to helping companies operate at a higher level.
